# Axos Bank Brand Bible & Creative Guidelines

## 1. Core Identity & Strategic Positioning
* **Mission:** To empower consumers and businesses to easily manage their financial lives through a blend of human insight and digital expertise, providing convenient, customized solutions that keep pace with a constantly changing world.
* **Brand Positioning:** "Banking Evolved." Axos is a technology-driven financial services company that has been digital from day one (founded July 4, 2000). The brand provides absolute freedom from the barriers and overhead of traditional brick-and-mortar banking.
* **Brand Archetype:** The Innovative Expert. Dynamic, insightful, and independently innovative, dedicated to providing unbeatable value and robust security.

## 2. Voice, Tone, and Copywriting Mechanics
* **Tone:** Professional, highly reliable, forward-thinking, clean, and empowering. Convey the security of a legacy bank with the agility of modern fintech.
* **Vocabulary Rules:**
    * Clear, direct language. Minimize dense banking jargon or explain it immediately.
    * Prioritize verbs of momentum: Evolve, Manage, Grow, Unlock, Accelerate, Thrive.
    * Focus on financial freedom, agility, and the power of compounding.
* **Prohibited Phrasing:** Do not use "traditional bank," "branch," or phrases implying physical banking constraints unless drawing a direct, negative contrast.
* **Virtual Assistant (Evo):** Helpful, exceptionally concise, responsive, and approachable without casual slang.

## 3. Visual Identity & Algorithmic Design System
* **Primary Brand Color:** Casablanca Orange (#FAA74A) — used for the "x" in the axos logo and key highlights.
* **Secondary/Accent Color:** Dark teal/blue — used for headlines, CTAs, and key text elements (e.g., "Get Axos ONE" buttons).
* **Backgrounds:** Clean light off-white / very light gray. Single-color only. Gradients strictly prohibited.
* **Typography:** Montserrat (Geometric Sans-Serif).
    * Headings/Display: Montserrat ExtraBold or Black.
    * Body: Montserrat Regular or Light.
* **Logo Guidelines:** Minimalist, professional. Maintain clear space equivalent to the height of the "A" in the Axos lettermark. Do not attach slogans directly to the logo lockup.
* **Grid System:** Bento-style grid with mathematically consistent rounded-corner containers for text blocks, data visualizations, and imagery.

## 4. Photography & Generative Imagery Art Direction
* **Style:** Authentic, slice-of-life lifestyle photography. Kinetic and genuine.
* **Lighting:** Bright, natural daylight.
* **Subjects:** Diverse, unposed humans captured in genuine business or personal activities. Short depth of field with blurred foreground/background to focus on the primary subject.
* **Strictly Prohibited:** Artificial studio backdrops, exaggerated facial expressions, ID badges, lab coats, overly stiff corporate suiting unless contextually required.

## 5. Approved Messaging Pillars
* **Pillar 1:** High-Yield Growth – "Make the most of your money with competitive APYs that help your savings grow faster through the power of compounding."
* **Pillar 2:** Zero Fee Agility – "No monthly maintenance fees. Bank fees? No thanks. Keep more of your money where it belongs."
* **Pillar 3:** Unrivaled Digital Access – "Born digital-first. Enjoy banking anytime, anywhere with the Axos all-in-one mobile app."
* **Pillar 4:** Security at Scale – "Expanded FDIC coverage up to $2.5MM. Your money stays protected with enterprise-grade security."
